  • Every person is once а player and than once a judge (option 1). Every person should have two votes to participate in one game.

  • One vs One mode:

  • Two players – one description
  • Both of the players have a pixel chart in front them on which they have to draw their ideas ... the given description
  • Option 1: After both the two players have finished their masterpieces the third person that is their judge in that game has 30 seconds to decide whose drawing is better.
  • Option 2: After both the two players have finished their masterpieces, the projects are exposed anonymously on the home page so that everybody can vote. After 30 seconds the votes are being counted. Of course if the votes are equal, a second round is played.
  • The most important part of the game is the joy!!!!!

  • Tournament mode:

  • First round – one description (easy – one word)
  • Again 2:30 minutes time for everybody
  • The drawings are being rated by one of our beautiful (handsome) superusers
  • Second round – second description (a little bigger)
  • 2 players and then the WINNEEEEEEEER!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!
